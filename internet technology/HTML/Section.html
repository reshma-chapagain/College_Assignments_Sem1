Google Chrome

Google Chrome is a cross-platform web browser developed by Google. It was first released in 2008 for Microsoft Windows, and later made available for Linux, macOS, iOS, and Android. Chrome is known for its speed, simplicity, and security features. It supports a wide range of web standards and technologies, making it a popular choice among users worldwide. Chrome also offers a variety of extensions and themes through the Chrome Web Store, allowing users to customize their browsing experience.
